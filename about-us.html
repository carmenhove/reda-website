About

Carmen Hove, PhD

Carmen has been making sense of complex datasets since 2015. As a research scientist at UC Santa Barbara, she conducted quantitative research on human health and served as the lead statistician on papers published in Nature Scientific Reports and Evolution, Medicine, and Public Health. After earning her doctorate, she worked as the lead data scientist at the National CASA/GAL Association for Children. Carmen started Reda Analytics in 2025, where she gets to work on interesting problems with diverse clients! You can find her on LinkedIn and GitHub.
